Research Areas

⚛️
Semiconductor Carrier Dynamics
Microwave photoconductance decay (μ-PCD) measurements of carrier lifetime and surface recombination velocity in LiTaO₃, perovskite materials, and oxide semiconductors. XPS surface characterization.
☢️
Heavy-Ion Nuclear Reactions
Statistical model simulations (PACE4) of fusion mechanisms, compound nucleus formation, and cross-section analysis for ²⁰Ne + ⁵⁹Co and ¹²C + ⁵⁹Co reaction systems.
🔋
Hydrogen Storage Materials
Development and characterization of MgH₂-based composites with ternary catalysts (TiH₂, TiO₂, Nb₂O₅). DSC, PCT, XRD, and SEM analysis of hydrogen absorption/desorption kinetics.
🌞
Perovskite Solar Cell Materials
Carrier trapping effects, interface/surface recombination in metal halide perovskites (MAPbI₃, MAPbBr₃, CsPbBr₃). CW laser mitigation strategies for accurate lifetime measurements.
🔬
Materials Testing & Characterization
X-ray photoelectron spectroscopy, X-ray diffraction, scanning electron microscopy, and time-resolved photoluminescence for advanced material characterization.
📐
Numerical Modelling
Phenomenological frameworks for nuclear reaction cross-sections, ambipolar diffusion modelling for carrier recombination dynamics, and Kissinger activation energy analysis.
